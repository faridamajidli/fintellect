Our History

Founded in 2025, Fintellect was created with the vision to empower students with the tools and knowledge to manage their finances effectively. Starting as a small project, we have rapidly evolved into a comprehensive platform that harnesses advanced analytics and AI-driven insights.

Our Values

At Fintellect, we believe in transparency, empowerment, and continuous learning. Our mission is to provide students with reliable financial insights and educational resources that promote responsible money management and long-term financial success.
